Bio Note

Michael Ernest Lorenz I. Cacho

Michael is a 3rd Year BS Computer Engineering Student of University of the East Manila (UE Manila). He's a free spirited individual that thrives to enjoy the present, but not at the expense of his future. Still not aware of his primary goal in life, he still eagerly jumps into opportunities to experience new things in life.

"In the end, it's not the years in your life that count. It's the life in your years." -Abraham Lincoln

Like any other students, his duties as a student is well above his priorities, but still below his thirst for finding new entertaining things to do. As he is aware of his laziness to do a lot of things, he delevoped a skill to find motivation in every little things he do in life. He now believes that there is more to gain in enjoying life one step at a time, rather than mulling over the difficulties ahead or the uncertainties the future hold.
